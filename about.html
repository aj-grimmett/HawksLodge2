The Hawks family consists of us Annette and Bill Hawks (Owners) and our twin boys Paul and Kyle. Our high rate of regulars (renters who return multiple times a year) are attributed to our efforts at provinding the best value on the trail system. We offer a great location, free reasonable amount of fire wood, moderen conviences, and a full bay workshop to keep your atv's on the trails all packaged in a wholesome family atmosphere.

Our motto is ride hard, ride safe, and have fun!!!!
