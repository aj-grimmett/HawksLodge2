The Hawks family is very proud of our lodging accommodations and wants to cater to our trail riders and families who are looking to have an enjoyable ride, a quiet relaxing place to stay, and the best service on the trail system. We will provide a more spacious modern facility with our duplex cabin-style lodge, while offering the view of the river, local wildlife, and being close to the best trail on the Hatfeild and McCoy trail system. Our lodge has a Verizon cell booster system, WIFI, 70 plus channels on TV, and access to an onsite washer and dryer.
