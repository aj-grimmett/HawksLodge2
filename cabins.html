The Hawks family is very proud of our lodging accommodations and wants to cater to our trail riders and families who are looking to have an enjoyable ride, a quiet relaxing place to stay, and the best service on the trail system.

We will provide a more spacious modern facility with our duplex cabin-style lodge, while offering the view of the river, local wildlife, and being close to the best trail on the Hatfeild and McCoy trail system.

Our lodge has a Verizon cell booster system, WIFI, 70 plus channels on TV, and access to an onsite washer and dryer.

The DEER is a two BEDROOM with a full and twin bed in one bedroom and a full bunk-bed and twin bed in the other room. The living room has a sleeper sofa whiches makes a full bed, and the kitchen is fully equipped.

The WOLF is a one bedroom with a queen and a twin bed. The master bath has a garden tub and shower, while the living room contains a sofa which makes a queen bed and a full-sized futon. The kitchen is also fully equipped with a side-by-side refrigerator.

We WILL GLADLY GIVE YOU ACCESS TO PREVIOUS RENTERS FOR QUESTIONS OR RECOMMENDATIONS!

RIDE HARD, RIDE SAFE, AND HAVE FUN!!!


HAWKS RIVERSIDE LODGE wishes everyone has a safe, enjoyable, and memory-making time. We want our families to feel safe and comfortable. If you are less experienced, we want to assist you in making your visit with us pleasurable.

Whether you are a first-time green trail rider or a more experienced black trail climber, we want you to be safe first and have lots of fun!!

OUR MOTTO: (RIDE HARD, RIDE SAFE, AND HAVE FUN) MEANS SAFETY FIRST, FAMILY AND FRIENDS HAVING FUN, AND MEMORIES BEING MADE.

The Hawks family feels blessed by our business growth and the friends we have made.
