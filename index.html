Our Location


HAWKS RIVERSIDE LODGE is only a short ride to an entrance of the ROCKHOUSE TRAIL SYSTEM. We are also centrally located between the towns of GILBERT and MAN. No need to pull your trailer around with you while being here, so you can hop on your ATV and go. The lodge is on private property on the Guyandotte River, which gives you the view of watching the blue herons fishing the shoals while you're enjoying the fire pit, or you could even try your luck with the local smallmouth yourself.

You can cook your meals on the charcoal grills provided or eat out, while you relax by the river. The lodge is very well equipped, with full-sized kitchens, a heat pump and ac, a washer and dryer, full-sized baths, and a spacious deck giving you the view of the river while you enjoy your morning coffee. Privacy and a family-oriented atmosphere are what give the HAWKS RIVERSIDE LODGE the highest return percentage on the trail system.

We also help riders plan their trip, listing what to bring, what weather to prepare for, and make every effort to keep your ATVs running with access to our workshop to make repairs if necessary. If we have nothing available for your dates, we will still help you plan your trip and recommend locations that will provide you with the service you desire.
